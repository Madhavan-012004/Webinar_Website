Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my certificate?
Certificates are automatically issued to your account after you complete a verified session. You can also manually claim them on the 'Certificates' page using your unique session ID.
Is MAD Academy really free?
Yes! Our core mission is to democratize education. Most live sessions and competitions are completely free to join.
How can I host a session?
Click the "Start Application" button on this page. Once approved by our team, you'll get access to the Host Studio.
